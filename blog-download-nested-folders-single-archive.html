Download Nested Google Drive Folders in a Single Archive

Complex Drive projects often include folders inside folders: drafts, finals, exports, and client-specific assets. A good downloader must preserve this structure when generating ZIP output. If hierarchy breaks, restoration becomes difficult and file context is lost.

When you archive nested folders as one file, you keep paths intact and reduce confusion after extraction. That is critical for engineering handoff bundles, media post-production pipelines, and design systems where directory layout matters.

Always verify after extraction that top-level and nested folders match the source project. One archive with correct structure saves hours of cleanup work.
