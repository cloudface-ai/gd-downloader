Backup Client Deliverables From Google Drive in One Click

Client folders usually contain final assets, source files, contracts, and revisions. Keeping only cloud links can be risky when permissions change or folders are edited later. A better approach is periodic archive snapshots.

Use one-click folder export to generate dated ZIP backups. Store them in your own project archive, NAS, or backup bucket. This preserves exactly what was delivered at that point in time and simplifies disputes, rework, and audit requests.

For agencies, a monthly backup cadence per active client can dramatically reduce operational risk with little overhead.
